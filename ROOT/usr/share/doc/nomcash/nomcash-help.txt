Well you managed to install nomcash, that's great!

With nomcash you have acquired a nice little GTK+ program to manage your money, be it cash or whatever.

You can create Pools which hold your money like your accounts, your pocket or whatever location you store it.

Basically, with nomcash you just protocol the money movements that occur between these pools. 

So that's it, folks!
